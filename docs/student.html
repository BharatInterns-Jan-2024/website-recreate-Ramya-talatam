Frequently asked questions

I’ve paid $99, why isn’t my premium subscription activated yet?

Your premium subscription will be activated once your school has reached 50 sign-ups! The sooner your school reaches 50 sign-ups, the sooner premium subscription will be unlocked!

I’ve previously participated in the back-to-school event but canceled my lifelong discount subscription, can I register again?

If you once got the lifetime discount at $99 annually, you will not be able to register for the event again to receive this discount again. As stated in the event details, the discount is lifelong for as long as you don’t cancel it. After you have canceled it, you can only resubscribe at the normal rate.

If I participate in this year's back-to-school event but hadn't reached 50 sign-ups, can I register next year for a better discount?

Sure. The event is open for anyone who has not enjoyed $99 lifetime discount yet. You can register next year to challenge the $99 annual subscription as long as your current plan is not $99.

What if my school doesn’t reach 50 people?

If your school doesn't reach 50 sign-ups by the deadline, you will get $99 full refund. You'll still have the chance to register next year to get the $99 discount.

In the remaining days, any user from that specific school who registers will continue to get that discounted price. For example: If your school reaches 50 people in 2 days, then every user who registers after will also get the premium subscription for $99 until the end of the event.

There will be no difference in the benefits you'll get. You'll enjoy exactly the same premium content and features, at a lower price!

How can I win the prizes for the referral event? Do I need to participate in the back-to-school event first?

You can refer friends to join LeetCode and win the prizes with or without signing up for the back-to-school event. Everyone will have their own exclusive referral link. We will track how many people sign up through your link, and you will be rewarded according to your count!
